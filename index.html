Qi Gong

Flow your body like water

Slowly becoming popular in europe, the Chinese practice has so many benefits to your body and health.

What is Qi Gong? It means 'energy cultivation' which focuses on body movements that activate the chi within us to improve your body, mind and spiritual being. It is in fact a form of medicine due to its variety of benefits by letting our chi flow through our meridians therefore dealing with blockages within our body which benefit the organ systems and illnesses.
